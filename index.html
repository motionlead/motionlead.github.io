Getting started

The MotionLead framework is composed of two classes :

  • MLManager : best suited for apps built with UIKit. Once launched, the MLManager is entirely autonomous and will display rich ads on your application when one is available.
  • MLView : provide more control on the way the ad is displayed. It is a subclass of UIView and it allows you to display ads on specific screens of your app.
